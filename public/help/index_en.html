To get the most out of your experience, we offer you this quick guide:

1. Activate GPS

For a personalized experience, make sure you have GPS enabled on your device. This will allow us to show your position on the map and provide you with relevant information about nearby historical sites.

2. Allow location access

Make sure you have allowed your browser to access your location. This will ensure that the app can provide you with an interactive and dynamic experience by displaying your real-time position on the map.

3. Explore the sights

Once your location is activated, explore the historical sites around you. The map will automatically adjust to show fascinating landmarks within 10 kilometers around you.

4. Real-time tracking

If you have allowed access to your location and have GPS enabled, the map will update in real time based on your movement. This way you can enjoy a unique and personalized exploration experience!

Frequent questions

How do I activate GPS on my device?
- To activate GPS on your device, go to location settings and make sure it is enabled.

How do I allow access to my location in the browser?
- Depending on your browser, you may find this option in your privacy or security settings. Make sure you give permissions so we can give you the best experience.

Enjoy exploring the history around you! If you have any additional questions, please feel free to contact our support team.
